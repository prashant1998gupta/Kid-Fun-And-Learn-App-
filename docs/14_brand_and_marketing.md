# 14 — Brand, Logo, Splash, Onboarding & Marketing Assets

## Brand
- **Name:** KidVerse. **Tagline:** *Play. Learn. Grow.*
- **Personality:** warm, magical, encouraging, safe, smart.
- **Voice:** cheerful, simple, second-person ("You did it!"). Never sarcastic,
  never scary.

## Logo
Wordmark "KidVerse" in **Baloo 2 ExtraBold**, violet→coral gradient, with a
small orbiting star dotting the "i" (nods to the universe theme). Icon mark: a
rounded planet with a smile + a ring of stars, usable standalone as the app
icon. Provide: full-color, mono, dark-bg, and 1024² app-icon variants.
Prompt: *"app icon, a cute smiling planet with a star ring, violet and coral
gradient, flat with soft 3D, rounded, thick outline, centered"*.

## Splash & onboarding (implemented)
- **Splash** (`splash_screen.dart`): space background, owl mascot elastic
  pop-in, wordmark rise, routes by profile existence.
- **Onboarding** (`onboarding_screen.dart`): 3 voice-narrated pages (Welcome /
  Play & earn / Grow together) with mascots + world backgrounds, dot indicator,
  Skip.

## Marketing copy
- **Short (Play):** "Where playing is learning! 100+ games, LKG–Grade 5. Reading,
  math, science & more — safe, ad-free, and offline. Kids play, parents smile."
- **Keywords:** kids learning, preschool, kindergarten, ABC, 123, phonics, math
  games, educational games, toddler, montessori.

Feature graphic (1024×500), promo video (15–30s screen-capture of the loop +
